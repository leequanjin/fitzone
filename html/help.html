Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to clear your doubts!

Signing up as a member is easy! You can visit our gym in person and our friendly staff will assist you with the registration process. Alternatively, you can also sign up online through our website "Lessons" section and complete the process from the comfort of your home.

Yes, we offer a complimentary one-day trial for anyone interested in experiencing our gym facilities and classes. It's a great opportunity to get a feel for our environment and the range of equipment we have available.

Membership transfers are generally not permitted. Memberships are intended for individual use and are non-transferable. However, if you have unique circumstances, we encourage you to reach out to our membership services team to discuss your situation and explore potential options.

Yes, you can generally upgrade or modify your membership plan. If you wish to change your membership plan, please contact our membership services team, and they will guide you through the process and any associated changes in pricing or benefits.

You can schedule training sessions by contacting our instructors directly or through our booking system online at profile section.Pick your preferable time slot and your application of booking slot is good to go.

We strive to provide the best possible experience for all our members. If you're not satisfied with your membership for any reason, we encourage you to first discuss your concerns with our staff or management. Refunds are subject to the terms and conditions outlined in our membership agreement.

We take cleanliness and hygiene seriously to provide a safe and comfortable environment for all our members. Our gym equipment and facilities undergo regular cleaning and maintenance. High-touch surfaces, such as machines, handles and common areas are cleaned multiple times throughout the day.

Yes, we have muktiple parking spaces available for gym members. Our parking area is well-lit and monitored for your safety and convenience.

Yes, we provide personal lockers for our members to store their belongings while they work out. However, please note that locker availability may vary based on demand. Lockers are intended for daily use only, and any items left overnight will be removed.

You can stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media platforms, and regularly checking our website for announcements about upcoming events, promotions, and special offers.
