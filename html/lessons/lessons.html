    Healthy Maker

    HIIT

    Tabata
    Within the first round of tabata training, your body enters the fat-burning zone and remains there for hours thereafter. 20 seconds of maximum effort are given throughout this exercise, followed by 10 seconds of recuperation. Although it seems simple, you will undoubtedly feel exhausted after this.
    Full-body HIIT
    Want to start a HIIT workout? Full-body HIIT will be your first choice. This type of interval exercise is better for weight loss and muscular toning than a protracted cardio workout.
    HIIT for Runners
    Do you still believe this kind of activity is required to navigate a large area? NOT AT ALL! Come learn about HIIT for runners, which can improve your stamina and endurance on race day.

    Yoga

    Ashtanga
    This is a kind of "Vinyasya Yoga". You will notice during the session how breathing and movement are synchronized to allow you to transition from one pose to the next.
    Hatha Yoga
    A beginner in yoga? There's no need to panic; Hatha yoga courses are the greatest option because they are often slower-paced than other yoga forms.
    Lyengar Yoga
    You will get the opportunity to try out various stances here, which require sustained effort. Even though you didn't move, you'll feel incredibly relaxed and open after class.

    Dance

    Zumba
    Zumba is a popular and energetic dance fitness program that combines dance moves with aerobic exercise. People of all ages and fitness levels appreciate the Zumba workout because it is made to be engaging and fun.
    Belly Dance
    Belly dance is a traditional and expressive form of dance that has a rich cultural history and has evolved over centuries. It is distinguished by fluid hip, torso, and arm motions as well as complex isolations and shimmies.
    Line Dance
    Join this low-impact class by entering one of our facilities. Work your core during class to make your entire body more flexible and coordinated.
